≡Expense categories

Group related costs (e.g. Subscriptions, Housing). The Actual column is optional — leave it blank and the planned amount is assumed. Want the deep view of where money goes? Open the Breakdown tab.

⚡One-off expenses · this month only

Spontaneous, single expenses that should not carry to next month (a gift, a repair, concert tickets…). Quick to jot down — they still count toward this month's spending and the breakdown.

◷Weekly spending money

📈Investments

Connect a savings pool to track it as an investment. The app already knows how much you put in and when (from your finalized months) — you just keep its current value up to date, and it works out your real returns.

🏦Pools — manage & history

Create pools, set their monthly target and end goal (or leave it indefinite), set a starting balance, and see every transaction across all time. Day-to-day deposits and withdrawals for a month are done on the Budget tab.

🎯Savings target

A quick calculator — not linked to your real budget. Try an income and a savings rate to see the target. (Your real per-pool targets stay on the Budget tab.)
